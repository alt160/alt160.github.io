Weird (but True) Coincidence

In early computing terminology, "2 bits" referred to 1/4 of a dollar, often used in the context of old arcade games or pricing in the past. Interestingly, in the world of digital data, "2 bits" also equals 1/4 of a byte. This charming coincidence highlights how some tech terms can have quirky overlaps with everyday language. It’s a small but fascinating example of how the world of technology intersects with the world of old-school lingo!
